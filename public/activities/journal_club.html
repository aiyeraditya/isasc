Science Communication Journal Club

What’s one way to improve the knowledge of science communication research among SciComm practitioners? Well, we have journal clubs in our own labs to become familiar with the most important research in our own fields; so why not do the same with SciComm research?

Enter the SciComm Journal Club, a monthly online discussion of a paper in the field of science communication research. The discussion begins with an overview for those that were not able to read in advance, followed by a facilitated discussion of the paper.

This activity is organized jointly with The Science Basement, a SciComm organization based in Helsinki, Finland. More Details
